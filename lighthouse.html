Lost at Sea

Still frame from 3ds Max. Post processing in Photoshop for minimal color grading.

Mix of procedurally generated shaders (for the ocean) and bitmap based shaders for base diffuse/normal/spec, with procedural ramps for the appearance of wetness.



Detail

Detail shot of the hi-res geometry. Landmass and trees are instanced sets of geometry prefabs to increase viewport responsiveness and render time.



Wireframe

Detail shot of the low-res geometry pre-shader/mapping. Normals/Specular from hi-res baked onto low-res geometry for final still frame.
