Penguin Poster

Poster began with a few initial sketches. Because it was being created as a gift, I immediately tossed out

the middle sketch. I found the penguins on either end to have more appeal in silhouette form.



With two penguins in different styles mocked-up in Illustrator, I decided to throw away the first penguin.

I found the second penguin's personality and subtle heart-shape more inviting than the first penguin's slightly ornery look.



Once I had the penguin selected, all that was left was the background and a few small tweaks. Final print size of 24x36in.
