Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 14, 2021

Thanks for visiting my portfolio site & blog! This website is published using Jekyll, jQuery, popper.js, lightbox.js, Bootstrap, and GitHub pages.

I believe in strong user privacy protections when browsing the web. Therefore, I have not added any trackers, ads, cookies, or analytics tools to my blog, nor do I share any information with third parties. The only information I may receive is content you've willingly sent to me via the e-mail links found throughout the site.

While your visit is entirely anonymous to me, GitHub may collect minimal information (such as IP addresses, timestamps, referring pages, strictly-necessary cookies, etc) to serve the content to you. Here is a link to GitHub's Privacy Policy.

The full source code for this site is available through my GitHub.
